## üìñ About The Mod

**Better Weapons** is a gameplay overhaul that expands weapon mechanics while maintaining the game's **vanilla styling**. The goal is to make combat more skill-based and dynamic without introducing items that feel out of place in the Hytale world.

Currently, the mod focuses on mobility for light weapons. The standard "Block" is replaced with a **Tactical Dash**:

| Weapon | Ability  | Description  |
| :--- | :---: | :--- |
| üó°Ô∏è **Daggers** | **Quick Dash** | Short rapid dash in the movement direction for backstabs. |
| üèπ **Bows** | **Evasive Step** | Short rapid dash to maintain distance from melee enemies. |

*Instead of blocking, daggers and bows allow you to perform a short dash for dodging or rapid closing.*
